Hello. We are EdgeFellows ,

a pledge fund for the initial phase for B2B deeptech technology projects.

We are network

Formed by a network of professionals who are passionate about high-impact technology and entrepreneurship who help early-stage Deep Tech Startups to reach the market and obtain financing to succeed in their projects.

Our focus

We focus exclusively on supporting startups working on deep emerging technologies such as machine learning, deep learning, machine vision, NLP, advanced materials, and quantum computing.

Our goals

Our goal is to identify high-potential technology companies and accelerate them to market, while supporting investors in their search for the best investment opportunities in the field of deep technologies.

For startups:

Technological mentoring: we have access to a wide range of professionals in the field of deep technologies to support the development of high-impact projects. Access to clients: We have access to a wide range of potential clients in the field of Health, Industry, Energy and Financial Services at a global level, we help startups to find their first client. Access to financing. We have access to a network of Business Angels and the best VCs from Europe and LATAM in the field of deep technologies. Access to research programs. We support companies in finding the best financing programs for their researchers

For investors:

Project dealflow. We give investors access to a huge number of high-tech projects in search of financing. Technological Due Diligence. We help investors in the Due Diligence phase with experts in the most varied fields of deep technologies, such as ai quantum computing algorithms and etic algorithms. Search for co-investors. We help in the search for co-investors for high-tech projects. Acceleration of your portfolio of companies. We help in the technological and business acceleration of your portfolio of high-tech startups.

Also...

Although we are agnostics of the sector, we bring a great knowledge in the field of HealtCare, Health Sciences, Energy, Industry. Through our network we support companies with high-level technological mentoring and advocacy, to connect clients for proof of concepts, to find financing for their projects, both public and private.
